MELISSA DODD-RUESING

The story of ChefMel
I have a passion for cooking.

I love to create. I am equally passionate about sharing my dishes with people for their utmost enjoyment. That passion is what lit my fire to create my own pasta sauce. What started as a desire to create my own sauce to fulfill my personal palate turned into a journey of sharing with friends, family and then the public. I brought my pasta sauce to work for a "potluck." We did this often, and once I brought my sauce, I no longer needed to sign up for a dish as one or many of my coworkers would sign up for me with a demand for my sauce. They were then requesting me to make sauce for them for them to purchase. With my entrepreneurial spirit, I fulfilled on their request and took it a step(or many) further and educated myself on "canning." I then began making large quantities and filling mason jars, even made cute little labels to make the jars look fancy or legit. I would bring in a case to work and would sell out in minutes. Back to the kitchen I went, and this continued on for a period of time until I decided to participate in a craft show. Making huge amounts of product, I sold it ALL and had to take orders and had people coming to pick up product from me or I would deliver cases. I did this for a while, working the "craft show circuit" and then I took it even further and decided to mass produce. I worked several farmers markets and worked my way into some small specialty stores.

I continued to grow, and partnered with a distribution company and currently have my "ChefMel's Sweet Sicilian Style Pasta Sauce" available at the 2 largest grocery chains in St. Louis, along with a few specialty stores, over 100 locations in MO and IL in total.
